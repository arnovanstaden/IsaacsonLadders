OUR COMMITMENT TO ENVIRONMENTAL AND SOCIAL SUSTAINABILITY

The production of aluminium is one the most environmentally friendly processes across the metals and mining industry. Aluminium can be endlessly recycled while keeping its distinctive properties.

At Isaacson Ladders we proud ourselves with being sensitive to the environment and acknowledge that climate change is affecting our daily lives. To reduce our impact on the environment and do our bit to combat climate change we have identified the material aspects associated with our operations that can impact on the environment.

We have introduced a Waste Management Program and actively recycle our major waste streams, thereby reducing our impact on landfill space. We are furthermore mindful of the way we use water and electricity. As for Social Sustainability, our staff compliment represents the diversity of the demographics of South Africa.
